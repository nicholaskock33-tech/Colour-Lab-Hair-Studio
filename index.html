About Colour Lab

At Colour Lab – Hair Studio, we combine science and style to create your perfect look.

Our expert stylists specialize in colour corrections, balayage, blondes, foils, and professional restorative treatments such as K18, Olaplex, and Brazilian Keratin.

We believe your hair should be as unique as you are — healthy, vibrant, and full of life.
